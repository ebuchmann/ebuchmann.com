Contact Me

I’m a painter currently living in Portland, Oregon. Between web development and painting I spend a good deal of my time working on creative things. My current motivation is to continue working with my fruit series of paintings while trying to improve my style and expression with each new piece.

If you’re interested in some art or just want to send me a message use the contact form. Some of my recent artwork is available for sale on Etsy and I’m also available for custom paintings.
