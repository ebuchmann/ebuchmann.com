Eric Buchmann

Impressionist Oil Painter

“Paint for fucking paint” as an old professor told me. To this date the statement still holds true in my work. I paint simply for the sake of painting while capturing a moment full of color and expression. In the end I strive to achieve my goal that every inch of the painting needs to be interesting.

About Me

As a painter I started working on artwork around 2003 when I enrolled in a painting course. As time went by I grew more interested in painting, and more specifically oil painting. I find the thickness of the paint to be a very important part of the overall painting and enjoy using a variety of bright colors. You can always contact me if you want to say something.
